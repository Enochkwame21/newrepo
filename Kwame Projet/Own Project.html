IT WILL HAPPEN

The word "Maranatha" is a Syriac expression that means: "our Lord comes." It was used as a greeting in the early church. When believers gathered or parted, they didn't say "hello" or "goodbye" but " Maranatha!" If we had the same upward look today l, it would revolutionize the church. O that God's people had a deepening awareness of the imminent return of the Savior!

SIGNS OF THE LAST DAYS
